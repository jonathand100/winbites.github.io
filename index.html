WinBites started the way the best ideas do — between two best friends who both happened to be named JD. We met in middle school, grew up obsessed with food and sports, and took that obsession with us to college.

One day between classes, we looked at what was in the snack aisle and asked: why doesn't anyone make a handheld pie that actually fuels you for game time? So we built one. Then three. Each one engineered for a different moment in your athletic day.

WinBites is our first product line — built under our parent brand JD², named for the two of us.

Why WinBites Works.

01
Moment-Matched

Pre, in, and post-game aren't the same moment. Your food shouldn't be either. Every WinBite is timed and tuned — potassium before, electrolytes during, protein after.

02
Real Flavors. Real Function.

Banana pudding, apple pie, peanut butter. Not flavoring — actual filling. The kind of food you'd eat anyway, engineered to do a job at game time.

03
Handheld. Always.

A sealed hand pie you can eat between drills, in the locker room, or sprinting to your next class. No bowls, no prep, no excuses. Performance food that fits your pace.

04
Sport-Coded by Design

Every SKU is tied to a sport and a moment in that sport. A baseball player warming up and a football lineman cooling down have completely different needs — and now, completely different pies.
